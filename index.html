Welcome

XaosRO (XRO) was a fan-made Ragnarok Online server, sponsored by The Lords of Chaos Clan, and was hugely popular in the mid to late 2000s. At its peak, the server maintained over 2000 players at a time.

Server Information
  • Rates: 3/3/3
  • Engine: eAthena
  • Specs: Dual Xeon 2.4 GHz, 4GB RAM, 100Mbps
